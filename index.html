BUKE — Artist Biography

Contact: Bukelive@gmail.com

BUKE is a Ugandan-born producer, recording pianist, and alternative music artist whose work dissolves the line between composition and emotion. Short for Bukenya, BUKE crafts sonic landscapes that live between silence and spirit—evocative, layered, and deeply immersive.

His sound merges elements of ambient, soul, experimental African rhythm, and cinematic storytelling. At the heart of BUKE’s artistry lies the piano—not just as an instrument, but as a vessel for memory, mystery, and movement. Every chord feels intentional. Every pause, sacred.

Growing up in a rich musical environment, BUKE developed a sensitivity to both silence and sound. His journey began as a pianist accompanying voices and bands but evolved into a deeper calling—producing genre-bending music that channels mood, shadow, and depth. His process is rooted in solitude, spirituality, and honest expression. Whether producing for others or performing live, BUKE invites listeners into intimate spaces—spaces where sound becomes ritual and emotion finds resonance. His music speaks to those searching for depth, healing, and truth. He’s here to evoke.

BUKE is currently preparing for a series of live performances, building toward his upcoming solo project. From studio to stage, he continues to shape his unique sonic fingerprint—one that resists categorization and leaves a lasting spiritual imprint.
